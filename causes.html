What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Healthy Food

Healthy food is key to building strong bodies and sharp minds, especially for children. It gives them the energy to grow, learn, and stay active while supporting their health and well-being. Advice from the Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation: Provide children with healthy meals, it’s a simple step toward a brighter, healthier future.

Pure Water

Pure water is essential for building strong bodies and sharp minds, especially for children. It keeps them hydrated, supports good health, and helps them stay active and focused. Advice from the Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation: Provide children with clean and safe water, it’s a simple step toward a healthier and brighter future.

Health Care

Quality health care is vital for building strong bodies and sharp minds, especially for children. It helps them grow healthy, prevents illness, and supports their overall well-being. Advice from the Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation: Ensure children have access to proper health care — it’s a simple step toward a healthier and brighter future.

Primary Education

It serves as a platform to empower children through access to quality foundational education, ensuring that they gain the necessary skills to read, write, and think critically from a young age.

Residence Facilities

These homes provide safe, supportive environments where children and youths receive care, education, and mentorship. Each location promotes growth, belonging, and a strong sense of community.

Social Care

The social care of the Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation is centered on creating a compassionate and inclusive environment where vulnerable children and youths feel valued and supported
