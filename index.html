Welcome to Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation
The Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation is a non-governmental organization (NGO) founded in 2022, dedicated to responding to humanitarian and development needs in Isoko North and South, Delta State, Nigeria. Since our inception, we have been working tirelessly to address the unique challenges faced by the Isoko community, providing both humanitarian assistance and development programs. We are registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) with registration number 7415943.
A prosperous and peaceful Isoko land where every individual has access to opportunities, resources, and support to thrive.
To empower Isoko youths and communities through education, healthcare, and economic empowerment programs, promoting peace, harmony, and sustainable development.

What We Do?

Healthy Food

Healthy food plays a vital role in building strong bodies and sharp minds, especially for children. It provides the energy needed to grow, learn, and stay active throughout the day. Nutritious meals help to strengthen the immune system, protect against illness, and support proper development. Beyond physical health, eating well also contributes to emotional well-being, helping children feel happier and more focused. A diet rich in natural, wholesome ingredients forms the foundation for a lifetime of good habits, encouraging a balanced and positive relationship with food. When children are given access to healthy meals, they are given a better chance to thrive in every aspect of life.

Pure Water

Pure water is essential for life and plays a key role in keeping the body healthy and functioning properly. It refreshes, cleanses, and helps maintain the balance of bodily systems, making it crucial for hydration, digestion, and overall well-being. Clean water supports clear skin, sharp thinking, and strong energy levels, allowing both children and adults to carry out their daily activities with ease. Beyond health, pure water also symbolizes safety and dignity, especially in communities where access to clean drinking water can transform lives. It brings peace of mind, knowing that each sip is free from harm and filled with the goodness nature intended.

Health Care

Health care is a fundamental part of human well-being, providing the support people need to live healthier, longer, and more fulfilling lives. It offers comfort and hope in times of illness, guiding individuals through recovery with compassion and expertise. Through regular care, people are able to prevent many diseases, manage existing conditions, and improve their quality of life. Health care also brings a sense of security, knowing that professional help is available when needed. It strengthens communities by ensuring that everyone, especially children and vulnerable individuals, has the opportunity to grow, learn, and thrive in good health.

Primary Education

The Primary Education of Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation is focused on nurturing the early academic growth of young children within the Isoko communities. It serves as a platform to empower children through access to quality foundational education, ensuring that they gain the necessary skills to read, write, and think critically from a young age.

Residence Facilities

The Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation offers residence facilities in Abuja, Ozoro, and France. These homes provide safe, supportive environments where children and youths receive care, education, and mentorship. Each location promotes growth, belonging, and a strong sense of community.

Social Care

The social care of the Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation is centered on creating a compassionate and inclusive environment where vulnerable children and youths feel valued and supported. Through various initiatives, the foundation addresses emotional, educational, and social needs, helping individuals build confidence and resilience.
