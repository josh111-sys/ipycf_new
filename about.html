Welcome To Isoko Progressive Youths Care foundation
The Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation is a non-governmental organization (NGO) founded in 2022, dedicated to responding to humanitarian and development needs in Isoko North and South, Delta State, Nigeria. Since our inception, we have been working tirelessly to address the unique challenges faced by the Isoko community, providing both humanitarian assistance and development programs. We are registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) with registration number 7415943.
Our History
The Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation was established to address the pressing needs of the Isoko community, including limited access to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities. Through our initiatives, we aim to bridge these gaps and create a more prosperous and equitable society for all.
At the Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation, we are guided by the following core values:
  • Compassion: We believe in showing empathy and kindness to those in need.

  • Empowerment: We are committed to empowering individuals and communities to take charge of their development.

  • Community-driven development: We believe in working with local communities to identify their needs and develop solutions that are tailored to their unique contexts.

  • Accountability: We are committed to transparency and accountability in all our operations.

    • Our Objectives
    The objectives of the Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation include:
  • To promote education and skills development among youth and vulnerable populations.

  • To improve access to healthcare and social services in Isoko.

  • To support economic empowerment initiatives that benefit local communities.
  • To advocate for the rights and interests of marginalized groups.
    • Through our programs and services, we have made a positive impact in the lives of many individuals and families in Isoko. We are committed to continuing our work and expanding our reach to benefit more people in the community.
    Join Us
    We invite you to join us in our mission to create a better future for the Isoko community. Whether through volunteering, donating, or partnering with us, your support can make a real difference in the lives of those we serve.
