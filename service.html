What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Healthy Food

Healthy food plays a vital role in building strong, healthy lives, and the Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation understands this importance. By providing nutritious meals to orphans, widows, the less privileged, and school children, the Foundation helps to improve their health, boost their energy, and support their overall well-being. Good nutrition strengthens the body, sharpens the mind, and gives children the strength they need to focus on their education and grow into healthy adults. Through this act of care, the Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation is not just feeding people but giving them hope for a brighter and healthier future.
