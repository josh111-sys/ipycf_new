Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation
Let’s come together and make a meaningful difference in the lives of others through the Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation. This foundation is fully committed to supporting orphans, widows, the less privileged, and those who are going through difficult times. Through acts of kindness and generosity, we can bring hope, happiness, and comfort to those who need it most. There are many ways you can support this mission. You can donate food items, clothing, educational materials, and healthcare supplies to help meet the basic needs of these vulnerable groups. Financial contributions are also greatly appreciated, as they help the foundation reach more people and expand its impact across different communities. Additionally, volunteering your time, skills, or services can make a lasting difference in the lives of the beneficiaries. The Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation believes that no act of kindness is too small. Together, through love, care, and support, we can uplift the lives of orphans, widows, and the less privileged, giving them hope for a brighter and better future. Join us today and be part of this great work that is transforming lives and restoring dignity to those in need.

Comrade Akpoturu Goodluck Ogheneyole

As the President General of Isoko Progressive Youths Care Foundation, I am proud to lead an organization dedicated to empowering the Isoko community through education, healthcare, and economic development initiatives. We believe in the potential of our people and are committed to providing opportunities for growth and development. Our foundation is driven by a passion to serve and a commitment to making a positive impact in the lives of our people. We will continue to work tirelessly to address the challenges facing our community and to build a brighter future for all. I invite all well-meaning individuals and organizations to join hands with us in this noble endeavor. Together, we can build a stronger, more prosperous Isoko community for generations to come. Let us work together to build a better future for Isoko.
